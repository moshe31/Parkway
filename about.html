OVERVIEWRedefining Microfinance Banking

PARKWAY MICROFINANCE BANK LIMITED is a leading Microfinance Bank offering comprehensive, affordable, dependable and specialized financial solutions to the enterprising Active Poor and burgeoning small and medium size enterprises. The Bank was incorporated in July 2007 and was granted License to commence microfinance banking business by the Central Bank of Nigeria on January 10, 2008.

Our goal is to reduce the population living below the poverty line by intervening and enhancing the capacities of the low end actively engaged populace which is in line with global, national and state initiatives of poverty alleviation. We continually focus on the unbanked and seemingly unbankable and convert these into the formal sector and viable businesses.

Our Goal Our goal is to reduce the population living below the poverty line

The Banks corporate head office is strategically located in the high density and trading nerve center of Lagos Island at 96/102 Broad Street, Mandillas Building, Lagos Nigeria. Since its inception, the Bank has been in the forefront of leading strategic product developments and tailor made services to alleviate the pains of the Active Poor and the enterprises. Our clientele base range from individuals to small and medium enterprises, with a motive to consciously create wealth to its identified clientele, especially the low end actively engaged populace.

MISSION

To be a vehicle in the global effort towards poverty alleviation by creating quality sustainable income generating opportunities for the low end actively engaged populace through the provision of solution based micro finance products and services.

VISION

To be the leading financial service provider to the micro-enterprise sector.
