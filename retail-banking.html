APPRENTICE FUTURE PLAN ACCOUNT

The account enables people under training in various vocational jobs (tailors, welders, hairdressers etc) to cultivate a savings habit.

  • N5,000 opening balance
  • Zero minimum daily balance
  • Zero monthly maintenance fee (subject to maximum monthly turnover)
  • No maximum monthly turnover
  • Allows deposit of cheques and dividends

Keep in mind that an account maintenance fee of N1/mile will apply on the excess, if the maximum turnover of N5 million is exceeded.
